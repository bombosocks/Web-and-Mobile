About Our Restaurant

We want you to have one of the most enjoyable and delicious dining experiences in the area. Our one-size 12” pie is just right for most people. Our basic topping is made of the finest crushed plum tomatoes, herbs, spices and a sprinkling of grated Romano & Parmesan cheeses. This creates a fresh flavor that’s true to the ingredients. We offer a variety of traditional and creative toppings to make each pie just the way you like it! We’re open seven days a week.
